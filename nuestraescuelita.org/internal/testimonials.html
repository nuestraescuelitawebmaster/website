Testimonios en dos idiomas / Testimonials in two languages

Nuestra Escuelita is a jewel of a preschool. My son has been attending Nuestra Escuelita for the past 2 years. He just turned 4 and his Spanish and English are amazing. He is clearly at or above grade level in both languages and effortlessly switches between English and Spanish depending on the children with whom he is playing. Nuestra Escuelita is a great preschool because it helps children to learn important social and self control skills in a loving environment. The teachers and parents who work with the children are fabulous. They treat all of the children as if they were their own. I cannot imagine a safer or more supportive environment for a child to develop. Having families of mixed cultures (e.g., Anglos and Latinos) and from many different parts of the world (e.g., U.S., Mexico, Colombia, Spain, Australia to name a few) has been a real blessing. My son, wife, and I have learned so much about Latin American and Spanish culture in the two short years we have been at Nuestra Escuelita, and we have made some life long friends. What more could one ask for? Anyone looking for a safe and supportive preschool where children learn in Spanish, are immersed in other cultures, and make wonderful friends-- Nuestra Escuelita is for you!
Glenn B., Parent and Professor of Child Development
Sunnyvale, CA
